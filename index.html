Research Projects

I'm broadly interested in working on Spatial AI for self-driving, robotics, and augmented reality applications. More specifically, I am interested in 3D reconstruction, mapping, visual localization, and uncertainty estimation. Some of my projects are listed below:

Improving 2D Map-based Visual Localization
Alan S Paul, Zador Pataki, Paul-Edouard Sarlin, Marc Pollefeys
Master Thesis, 2024
Slides / Thesis / Demo / Code

Semester Project, 2023

Neural network that estimates extrinsic (roll and and pitch) and intrinsic (focal length and distortion) camera parameters from a single image.
